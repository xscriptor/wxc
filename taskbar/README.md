<h2>Features</h2>
<p>
Custom corner radius and padding<br />
Transparent and acrylic backgrounds<br />
Theme-aware foreground (text and icons)<br />
Focus on readability and elegance<br />
Lightweight modifications that respect system UX
</p>

<h2>How to Use</h2>
<p>
1. Make sure <a href="https://windhawk.net/">Windhawk</a> is installed on your system.<br />
2. Copy any of the <code>.json</code> files into Windhawk's <strong>Notification Center Styler</strong> or <strong>Taskbar Styler</strong> module.<br />
3. Apply the style from Windhawk's configuration interface.<br />
4. Restart Explorer (if necessary) for the changes to take full effect.
</p>
